Privacy Policy

1. What do we do with your information?

At Unurgent, we respect your privacy to the very best of our ability. We only collect data that is strictly necessary in order for us to provide you with our messaging service. We never collect any data for the sole purpose of tracking you, or your usage of the app.

When you register in our Unurgent app for iOS, we collect the personal information that you provide us with during signup, such as your name and email address. When you use the app, we collect information directly related to your usage of the app. This includes, but is not limited to, the messages that you send, the chats that you join, the contacts that you add, and the invites that you create. With your permission (consent), we may send you emails about new products and other updates (email marketing).


2. Consent

How do you get my consent? If we wish to use your personal information for any reason not related to the functionallity of the app, such as marketing, we will either ask you directly for your expressed consent, or provide you with an opportunity to say no.


3. Disclosure

We may disclose your personal information if we are required by law to do so, or if you violate our Terms of Service.


4. Third-Party services

We never share your user data with any third party. Exceptions stated in section 3 apply.


5. Security

To protect your personal information, we take reasonable precautions and follow industry best practices to make sure it is not inappropriately lost, misused, accessed, disclosed, altered or destroyed. Although no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure, we follow generally accepted industry standards.

5.1 All data sent between our iOS app and our backend servers is encrypted using Secure Socket Layer technology (SSL).


6. Age of consent

By using the Unurgent messenger app, you represent that you are at least the age of majority in your state or province of residence, or that you are the age of majority in your state or province of residence and you have given us your consent to allow any of your minor dependents to use this service.


7. Changes to this privacy policy

We reserve the right to modify this privacy policy at any time, so please review it frequently. Changes and clarifications will take effect immediately upon their posting on the website. If we make any material changes to this policy, we will notify you, either in the app, or by email, that it has been updated, so that you are aware of what information we collect, how we use it, and under what circumstances, if any, we use and/or disclose it. If our service, or company, is acquired or merged with another company, your information may be transferred to the new owners so that we may continue to provide the service to you.
